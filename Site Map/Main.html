Crusaders of the Lost Idols Calculator and Comprehensive Reference Site

Introduction

The purpose of this site is to provide a one-stop reference for formation and dps calculattions. Ultimately the goal will be to provide automated advice on things like:

  • How to improve your designed formation.
  • Which choice of talents would best improve your current formation.
  • What formation design type might be ideal for your current gear.
  • Which crusaders might be ideal for a given mission, with the option of keeping crusaders for your formation rather than sending them on missions.

How to use the site

To utilize this site you will first want to go to the "Automated Input" section to input your data. This should guide you through the process of adding in all your crusader information so that the rest of the calculator can do it's thing.

Once you have added all the crusader content, you can go and see your crusader values here.
